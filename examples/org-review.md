# Org Review Example

Prompt:

```text
Use x-org and x-teams on this blocked project:
Engineering waits for Product, Product waits for Legal, Legal waits for Security, and nobody can name the final owner.
```

Expected focus:

- Direct owner-to-owner communication.
- Which approvals prevent real harm.
- Which steps are stale process.
- Whether the team has enough accountable builders.
