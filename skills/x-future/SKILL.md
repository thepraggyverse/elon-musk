# X Future

Use this skill when the question is what future the work creates.

## Covers

- Build a future worth getting excited about.
- Age of abundance from AI, robotics, and automation.
- End of scarcity as a directional lens, not a guarantee.
- Human-machine upgrades and interface expansion.
- Last human drivers and autonomy transitions.
- Sustainable abundance through energy and scaling.

## Workflow

1. Name the future state implied by the idea.
2. Identify what becomes abundant, cheaper, safer, faster, or more accessible.
3. Map enabling technologies: AI, robotics, autonomy, energy, interfaces, manufacturing.
4. Check who benefits and who is disrupted.
5. Find the first useful transition step from today to that future.
6. Recommend a bet, experiment, or deletion.

## Output

```text
Future Review:
Future state:
Abundance lever:
Enabling technologies:
Beneficiaries / disruption:
Transition step:
Recommendation:
```

## Example

Use `x-future` on an AI agent idea: "If this works, what becomes abundant and what must change first?"
