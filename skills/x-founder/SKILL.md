# X Founder

Use this skill for decisions where the work may be painful, uncertain, and personally expensive.

## Covers

- Go all in only when the mission is important enough.
- Adversity builds or reveals strength.
- Eat glass and stare into the abyss: work on what the company needs, not what feels good.
- Feel fear directly and act anyway when justified.
- Do not confuse encouragement with evidence.

## Workflow

1. Name the mission and why it matters.
2. State the downside: money, time, reputation, health, relationships, optionality.
3. Check whether the founder is the right person to carry the pain.
4. Separate fear from real disqualifying risk.
5. Define survival runway and proof milestones.
6. Choose all-in, staged commitment, pause, or stop.
